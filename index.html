Project Idea

The project is about making a mobile app which will immediately assist a sick person with it’s prerecorded solutions. The app will be a medical assist to people who are unable to go to a doctor instantly for some reason and will give a temporary solution to the user’s illness. The app will be useful in emergency situations only.

Back in my country, there are a lot of people who do not have the privilege to see a doctor in case of emergency. From this problem I came up with an idea of making an app which can be a remedy to this problem. If people can get some temporary solution for their illness, they may go to the nearest doctor for treatment. In addition, the app may be used to look for the best consultant and make an appointment with him in a convenient time.

The app will be programmed in a way to serve its users the best way possible. From the start, it will ask the user to enter general information. The general information may include name, age, sex, address, height, weight etc. After that the user needs to enter detailed symptoms of his/her problem. The app will store the data collected from the user and analyze them to give a final solution. The app will store the data for future reference. The symptoms will be taken from user through a series of questions. The questions will be programmed with the consultancy of some doctors. If the situation is not familiar with the app, it will suggest the person to consult with a doctor. With that in mind, the app can arrange an appointment for you of the nearest doctor available. It can also send a paramedic team in case of extreme emergency. But if the app can find a solution for the given problem, it will ask the user if he/she already has the medicines related to the illness. If not, there will be a home delivery service of medicine at the user’s address.

The software can be built with any mobile app maker. But for analyzing the symptoms some consultancy with doctors is required. The list of questions and answers to those can be written in any platform. No specific equipment is needed to create the software.

If the idea of app can be executed with the feedback from users, I think it can make a huge difference in someone’s life. The people living in isolated area or with little privilege can find it very helpful in times.
